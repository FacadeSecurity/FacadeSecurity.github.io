Many companies are without a Cyber Security Subject Matter Expert to oversee their company's digital presence. A CISO (Chief Information Security Officer) oversees a company's security as a whole. Sometimes it is not within business capabilities to hire someone for such a vital role. A CISO would help with securing infrastructure, provide Security Awareness Training, and create teams for for security operations as needed. We offer Virtual CISO services to clients in need of that Subject Matter Expert! With our help, we can create and implement a business strategy to further secure your company. We offer contracts for varying durations. A vCISO contract can be beneficial as we will manage all your security endeavors.
